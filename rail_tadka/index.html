About Us

About 6 billion passengers travel in Indian Railways each year. If anyone of us has traveled they will vouch for the fact that currently the availability of quality food in Indian Trains is something not to be excited about. Rail Tadka solves this very problem for the railway passenger.
It offers a variety of choices to a Railway passenger so that the passengers can order food as per their liking and at their price point while they are travelling. The Rail Tadka automated platform tracks trains in real time across India and ensures that fresh food is delivered to the passenger as per their choice in real time.
